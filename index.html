About Us

Jezter allows parents to personalize their child's screentime with a time controlled, password protected app, including a catalog of pre-selected content. From favorite movies to educational games, all content is fully customizable by the parent.  Jezter's hand-picked catalog is not limited to videoit includes all forms of children's media, including ebooks, educational web pages and music. Our founders realized that there was a demand for parental controlled entertainment for children and are committed to providing the user with the best possible experience they can have on their device. 

Mission Statement

Our mission is to entertain children with educational and age appropriate content, while giving parents peace of mind through complete control over their child's screentime.
